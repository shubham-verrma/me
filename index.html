Who I Am

Started at 19.
Built what most people only plan.

I built my first company at 19 while studying engineering at Manipal University. Aural was a social audio platform for India's next billion users. It failed. I did not care. Selected from 200,000+ applications, we pitched directly to Sony's management team at Shark Tank India Season 2 — and it taught me more about building than any degree ever could.

I ran a 30-person team at zero salaries. Filed trademarks. Secured Microsoft incubation. Shipped product. All before I graduated.

Since then I have operated at the intersection of GTM, growth, and product across a global neobank, a YC-backed creator economy SaaS, and multi-crore SME programs. I am now building Grantsetu, a GovTech platform unlocking ₹5 lakh crore in government subsidies for Indian manufacturers.

"I have failed, pivoted, shipped, and scaled. Experience was never going to wait for a degree."
